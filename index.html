PORTFOLIO

Rank and File

A political app with the aim to give voters a transparent look at the representatives of their state. The user of this app will be able to view the contact information, leadership roles, voting information, and latest news stories from a myriad of news sources of their state's current congressmen. This app is fully responsive and was built using HTML5, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.
